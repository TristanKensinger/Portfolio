Lead Engineer of NUKEMAP3D Reborn

The aim of the project is to demonstrate the devastating power of nuclear weapons in the hopes of deterring support for nuclear warfare. My responsibilities include managing the engineering team, modeling the nuclear explosion in Blender, accurately scaling the explosion and city based on real life data, exporting assets to Unity, and porting the finished product onto the web.
